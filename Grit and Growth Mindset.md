The video explains that growth mindset means you can improve your abilities through effort and practice.

##  Understanding Internal Locus of Control
Internal Locus of Control - It explains that how the actions,efforts can affect the results, not the luck.
The video's main key point is that people are motivated when they think that there efforts could change the results, instead of the luck.

## How to build a Growth Mindset
* Believing that skills can be developed, not fixed
* Embrace the difficulties
* See efforts as improvement
* View mistakes or failures as setbacks and learn them
* Being curious to learn new things
* Surrounding with growth-oriented mindset people
* Pushing ourselves beyound the comfort zone
