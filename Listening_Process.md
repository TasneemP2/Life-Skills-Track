# Listening_Process
## What is the Feynman Technique? Explain in 1 line.
Feyman Technique is the process of teaching yourself like you do to others to understand the concepts better.

## In this video, what was the most interesting story or idea for you?
The most interesting story for me in this video is how Barbara Oakley struggled to learn maths, and later trained herself as a slow learner, ended up being an engineering profesor.
She has showed that, learning is not about talent, but instead the consistency and the methods we apply.

## What are active and diffused modes of thinking?
Active Mode - When we are giving our full concentration to the solve something.
Diffused mode refers to open thinking, where our mind wanders to other topics which we are not focusing.

## According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic? Only mention the points.
* Break the skill into smaller sub-skills
* Learn just enough to self-correct
* Remove distractions and barriers
* Set a specific practice time
* Commit to at least 20 hours of practice

## What are some of the actions you can take going forward to improve your learning process?
* Paying full attention while learning.
* Reduce distractions by turning off the notifications.
* Ask each concept where it is used for better understanding.
* Set Personal deadlines to motivate myself.
* Explain concepts to myself to better understand how far i have learnt the topic.
