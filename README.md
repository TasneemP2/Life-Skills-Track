# Active Listening
## 1.Strategies to do Active Listening
Active Listening - The act of listening to someone and comprehending the meaning. Here are some strategies that help to improve our listening skills.
*  Avoid getting distracted by your own thoughts.
*  Focus on the speaker and the topic instead.
*  Try not to interrupt the person.
*  Let them finish and then respond.
*  Use some of phrases like Tell me more, Go head, I am listening, That sounds interesting to show that you are listening.
*  Show that you are listening through the body language.
*  If appropriate, take notes while discussing important conversations.
*  Pair the phrase, to be sure that you are on the same page.

## 2.The key points of Reflective Listening

### Listen Actively 
* Listen actively without interupting the speaker.
* Do not plan your response while someone is speaking.

### Acknowledge feelings
* Acknowledge the feelings behind the words.
* Show that you acknowledge the feelings with some phrases or the body language.

### Prephrase the message
* Repeat the speaker's message so that it shows that you are listening actively.
* It also helps you know that you are on the same page with the speaker.

### Reflect emotions and meanings
* Reflect the emotions the same as the speaker.

### Clarify and confirm understand
* If unclear, ask questions and understand the speaker.
* It shows that you are listening actively.
 
 ###  Avoid judging and evaluation
* Do not jump to the end.
* The goal is to understand, not to judge.
* Be patient while listening.

### Show Empathy
* Show that you geniuenly care by body language like nodding, eye contact.

### Focus on Interests, not positions
* Understand why the person feels like that, instead of judging.
